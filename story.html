Our Story

Forged in the fog

As was the case across the globe, gyms across San Francisco shut down during the early days of the COVID pandemic.

After a few months, a handful of stir-crazy people craving endorphins and human connection started coming together for outdoor runs... then swims... then track workouts.

Someone would bring a dumbbell. Someone else a few jump ropes. Maybe a kettlebell or two if we were lucky. Word spread. The group grew. People kept showing up — and then kept coming back.

Six years later, we're still here. In that time we've watched each other meet their partners, get married, and have kids. Then have more kids.

Turns out we didn't need a building or fancy equipment. The only thing we needed? A trusty Subaru Outback to schlep our gear.

But really — give us an abandoned building foundation and a group of people who share the same mentality: show up, work hard, have fun, repeat.

If that sounds like your kind of crew, come find us. New faces are always welcome.
